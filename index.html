FROM THE FRONTEND →

2 PLAYERS agree to play a game on the platform

Both players sign a smart contract with a challenge fee and a chess game instance is created for both players to enjoy.

← ON THE BACKEND

SMART CONTRACT acts as a proctor and escrow

Smart contract monitors the game and awards the winner 90% of the total pot amount. A donation fee (10% of the total pot amount) is donated to the Chess in Slums foundation's address

CHESS IN SLUMS

Raising Kings and Queens in the Slums

Chess In Slums is a Lagos, Nigeria-based grassroots charity whose mission is to empower impoverished children in the Nigerian slums of Lagos through chess education.
